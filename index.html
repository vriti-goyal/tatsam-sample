About Tatsam

Tatsam is a hindi literary society which focuses on promoting hindi amongst college students by giving them a chance to develop their talent in various hindi literature domains like shayari, kavita and many more. It is one of the most active societies in nsut which organises regular debate competitions, group discussions, poetic gatherings etc.
